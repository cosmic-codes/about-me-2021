I'd like to make a note for those who've seen the project dates on my resume and LinkedIn--though this project was time-consuming, it wasn't difficult enough to consume three years. Here is a general development timeline of when things happened:

  • 2018: Project concept, unpublished beta version. 75% of the working code.
  • 2019: Researched and refined code from a Japanese example. 25% of the working code.
  • February 2020: Began commission work with CelestiaKnight to obtain images for the project's front end.
  • May 2020 to May 2021: Project hiatus amid family/financial struggles, design team research, and major death in my family.
  • June 2021: Gathered the commission work to assemble the front end, begin processing code/dictionaries for official release.

I wanted to ensure that I was transparent about the time frames. I take my projects seriously, and did not want to give the impression that it took three years in total to reach this level of progress.
